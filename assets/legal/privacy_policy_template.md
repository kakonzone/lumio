**Last updated:** [DATE]

## Data we collect

- **Device identifiers:** install ID, device fingerprint (for ad caps and fraud prevention)
- **Advertising ID (GAID):** when personalized ads are enabled
- **IP address** and coarse location (from ad networks)
- **App usage:** channels viewed, ad impressions (aggregated)
- **Crash logs:** Firebase Crashlytics (non-PII where possible)

## Why we collect it

- Deliver live TV streams and related content
- Show and measure ads (app is free with ads)
- Prevent fraud, VPN abuse, and cap policy violations
- Improve stability and fix crashes

## Ad partners

We work with:

- **Unity LevelPlay / IronSource** (may mediate Unity Ads)
- **Adsterra**
- **Monetag** (PropellerAds family)
- **Firebase** (Analytics, Remote Config, Crashlytics, Messaging)

Each partner has its own privacy policy. You can limit personalized ads in **Menu → Ads & privacy**.

## Retention

- Ad telemetry and caps: typically 24–90 days on our servers
- Install ID: until app uninstall or data deletion request
- Crash logs: per Firebase default retention
