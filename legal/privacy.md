# Lumio TV — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

## Data we collect

- **Device identifiers:** install ID, device fingerprint (ad caps and fraud prevention)
- **Advertising ID (GAID):** when personalized ads are enabled
- **IP address** and coarse location (from ad networks)
- **App usage:** channels viewed, ad impressions (aggregated)
- **Crash logs:** Firebase Crashlytics

## Why we collect it

- Deliver live TV streams and related content
- Show and measure ads (the app is free with ads)
- Prevent fraud, VPN abuse, and policy violations
- Improve stability and fix crashes

## Ad partners

We work with **Unity LevelPlay / IronSource**, **Adsterra**, **Monetag**, and **Firebase** (Analytics, Remote Config, Crashlytics, Messaging). Each partner has its own privacy policy. You can limit personalized ads in **Menu → Ads & privacy**.

## Stream caching

The app may cache playlist metadata and short-lived stream tokens on device storage to reduce load times. Cached data is cleared during storage maintenance or when you clear app data.

## Retention

- Ad telemetry and caps: typically 24–90 days on our servers
- Install ID: until uninstall or a deletion request
- Crash logs: per Firebase default retention

## Your rights

Depending on your region you may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data.

**Contact:** legal@lumio.app

## Children

Lumio is not directed at children under 13.
